## Judging model

Stage One is a pass/fail check for theme fit and reasonable use of the required tools. Stage Two equally weights:

1. Technological Implementation — genuine, non-trivial Codex-built implementation;
2. Design — complete, coherent and runnable product experience;
3. Potential Impact — credible solution for a specific real audience;
4. Quality of the Idea — creativity, novelty and problem understanding.

Technological Implementation is the first tie-breaker, followed by the criteria in listed order. The official rules currently list judging through August 5, while another schedule page differs; the official rules control and public artifacts will remain available beyond both dates.
